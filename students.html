How to Manage the Stresses of Social Media

Source: spunout.ie

  1. The battle for likes: Social media can be fun but can also lead to disappointment. Focus on living your life offline and taking breaks from social media when needed.
  2. Challenge unhelpful thoughts: Practice mindfulness to challenge negative self-comparisons.
  3. Comparing yourself to others: Remember, social media shows a curated "best version" of people's lives.
  4. False images: Not all pictures online are authentic. Keep this in mind to avoid unrealistic comparisons.
  5. You're more than your profile: Engage in real-life connections by texting or calling friends.
  6. Switch off for a while: Use apps to limit your social media usage and regain control.
  7. Take control of your feed: Follow people who uplift you and unfollow those who donâ€™t.
  8. Cyberbullying: Report or block users who post mean or hurtful comments.
  9. Connect offline: Meet friends in person and enjoy real-life interactions.
